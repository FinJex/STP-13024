Privacy Policy

Who we are

Our website address is: openly.it.com

What personal data we collect and why we collect it

The personal data we collect will be used for the following purposes:

  • To respond to emails from users
  • To send product information, if users have opted in to mailing list communications
  • We require an opt-in for consent to store personal data
  • To inform users about products and respond to inquiries

Cookies

If you leave a comment on our site, you may opt-in to saving your name, email address and website in cookies. These cookies are for your convenience so that you do not have to fill in your details again. These cookies will last for one year. If you have an account and log in, we will set a temporary cookie to determine if your browser accepts cookies. This cookie contains no personal data and is discarded when you close your browser. When you log in, we will set cookies to save your login information and display preferences. Login cookies last for two days, screen options cookies last for a year. If you select "Remember Me", your login persists for two weeks. Logging out removes login cookies. If you edit or publish an article, an additional cookie is saved in your browser. It contains no personal data and expires after one day.

Embedded content from other websites

Articles on this site may include embedded content (videos, images, etc.). Embedded content behaves the same as visiting the external website directly. These websites may collect data, use cookies, and track interactions, including if you are logged into those websites.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to gather visitor data. This information is not shared with others.

Who we share your data with

By default, we do not share any personal data with anyone.

How long we retain your data

Comments and their metadata are retained indefinitely to allow automatic approval of follow-ups. Registered users can view, edit, or delete their personal information at any time. Administrators can also access and edit this information.

What rights you have over your data

You can request an exported file of your personal data or request its deletion. This excludes data required for legal or administrative purposes.

Where we send your data

Visitor comments may be checked through an automated spam detection service.

Your contact information

To contact us, please send email at support@openly.it.com

Consent

By consenting to this privacy policy, you allow us to process your personal data for the specified purposes. Consent must be explicitly given. You may withdraw consent at any time via email support@openly.it.com.

Disclosure
